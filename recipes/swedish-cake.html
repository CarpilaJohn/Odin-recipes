Swedish Cake

Description

Ingredients
  • 2 medium zucchini
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 jar marinara sauce
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Slice the zucchini into thin strips.
  3. In a skillet, brown the ground beef over medium heat. Drain excess fat.
  4. Add marinara sauce and Italian seasoning to the beef. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. In a baking dish, layer zucchini, ricotta cheese, meat sauce, and mozzarella cheese.
  6. Repeat layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with mozzarella and Parmesan on top.
  7. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until cheese is bubbly and golden.

This zucchini lasagna recipe might be low in carbs, but it’s high in flavor!
